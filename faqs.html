FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What services does DNC Events provide?

We offer professional event styling, hosting, catering, and event organizing services for weddings, birthdays, christenings, and kiddie birthday party.

How do I book an event?

You can book your event by visiting our booking page, filling out the form, and submitting your details. Our team will contact you shortly to confirm.

Do you provide customized packages?

Yes! We tailor packages according to your needs, budget, and the type of event. Contact us to discuss your details.

What areas do you cover?

We serve events across Metro Manila, Rizal, and nearby provinces. For special locations, please contact us for availability.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least a month or more in advance, especially for weddings and large events, to ensure availability and proper planning.
